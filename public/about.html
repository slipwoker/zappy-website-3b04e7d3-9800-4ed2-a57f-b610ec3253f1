Meet Howard Kessler, DC

A trusted chiropractor in Bergen County, NJ, dedicated to helping patients of all ages and walks of life move, feel, and live better.

A practice built on trust and connection

With years of hands-on experience serving the Bergen County community, Howard Kessler, DC brings a philosophy of care that goes beyond simple adjustments. He believes that effective chiropractic treatment starts with truly listening to each patient's story — understanding their daily routines, physical demands, and personal health goals.

Whether you're a weekend athlete recovering from a sports injury, a working professional dealing with back pain, or a senior looking to maintain mobility and independence, Dr. Kessler tailors every treatment plan to your unique needs. His approach integrates gentle, evidence-based techniques with a genuine respect for the body's natural ability to heal.

From herniated discs to spinal decompression and everyday aches, his goal is to help you find lasting relief — not just a quick fix. He is proud to serve patients from age 10 to 100, creating a welcoming environment where everyone feels heard, respected, and empowered on their journey to wellness.

Proudly serving Hillsdale and all of Bergen County, NJ.

Our Practice Philosophy

Guided by three simple principles that put your well-being at the center of everything we do.

Care That Feels Personal

Every patient who walks through our door is treated like family. We take the time to listen, understand your unique story, and craft a care plan that addresses your specific needs — never a one-size-fits-all approach.

Guidance That Fits Real Life

Healing doesn't stop when you leave our office. We provide practical, realistic guidance that integrates seamlessly into your daily routine — whether you're a weekend warrior, a busy professional, or enjoying your golden years.

An Inclusive Environment for All

From age 10 to 100, our practice proudly serves the entire Bergen County community. We celebrate diversity in age, background, and lifestyle, creating a welcoming space where everyone feels comfortable and respected.
