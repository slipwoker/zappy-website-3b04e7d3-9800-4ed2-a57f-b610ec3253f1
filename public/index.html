Meet Dr. Howard Kessler

Since earning his Doctor of Chiropractic from Life Chiropractic College in 1986, Dr. Kessler has dedicated his career to understanding what's really causing your discomfort — not just masking symptoms. As a Certified Chiropractic Sports Physician (New York Chiropractic College, 1991), former adjunct professor of Anatomy & Physiology at Bergen Community College, and twice voted the Pascack Valley's top chiropractic physician by (201) Magazine (2015, 2016), Dr. Kessler brings uncommon depth to every patient he meets. His approach is simple: look deeper, listen carefully, and design a personalized plan that addresses the spinal imbalances often at the root of your pain. Under his care, patients consistently report moving more freely, waking with less stiffness, and returning to the activities they love — whether that's a weekend game, a pain-free workday, or simply playing on the floor with grandchildren.
